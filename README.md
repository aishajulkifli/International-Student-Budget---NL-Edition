# International Student Budget - NL Edition

A comprehensive budget planning guide for international students studying in the Netherlands.

## Overview

This project provides practical budgeting resources and tools for international students to manage their finances while studying in the Netherlands.

## Features

- Budget templates
- Expense tracking
- Cost of living estimates
- Financial planning tips

## Getting Started

1. Review the budget templates
2. Customize amounts based on your situation
3. Track your expenses regularly

## Resources

- Housing costs
- Food and groceries
- Transportation
- Entertainment and leisure
- Education expenses
